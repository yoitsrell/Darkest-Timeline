The Batman Who Laughs

The Batman Who laughs start out just like the regular Batman.

The Joker decides to launch his endgame. He kills all of Batman's other villains and the police force.

Batman finally snaps and kills the Joker.

The Joker, in his final moments, poisons Batman with a toxin that causes him to slowly act like the Joker.

Noticing the changes in Batman, the Bat-family comes to his aid. While they were looking for options to help, Batman completely submits to the Joker's poison and kills his allies.

He spares and "jokerizes" his own son

One week later, he destroys the Justice League

He continues his reign of terror over the world. He takes down every opposing force. He eventually joins the evil god Barbatos and leads the Dark Knights of the Multiverse. They proceed to destroy multiple realities.
